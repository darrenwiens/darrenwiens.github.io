Gourdle!

Sat 12 March 2022

Launched independently in October 2021, Wordle, now owned by NY Times, has spawned many clones. In fact, my morning routines currently features:

  • Wordle (guess one word!)
  • Quordle (guess four words!)
  • Heardle (guess one song!)

Surprisingly, to my delight, as far as I can tell, there is not yet a game called Gourdle1, a gourd guessing game! I've made a prototype here, with a hardcoded value for a gourd image (making the image dynamic will be part of phase 2, once I've secured funding).

1: There are rumours of a Pokemon named Gourdle, however it doesn't seem to be in what I assume is the official Pokedex, which I interpret as a legal document, so I should be good from an ownership perspective.
